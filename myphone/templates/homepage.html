samsung s21 ultra






You get a better main 108MP camera, a glass back (instead of plastic), more RAM and a higher-res display. As you'll see in my Galaxy S21 Ultra review, there are some trade-offs. But overall, this is one of the best phones money can buy for those willing to pay a premium and one of the best Android phones overall.

Oneplus 9R




With only cosmetic changes and a slightly upgraded processor compared to the OnePlus 8T, the new OnePlus 9R is not very exciting at first. It has been launched exclusively in India because the OnePlus 9 and 9 Pro have come in at higher prices. The company could have done more to keep things fresh in the premium segment, but even so, the 9R is a good overall package. It looks slick, and has a crisp AMOLED screen plus stereo speakers. Performance is good in everyday tasks and games run well too.
